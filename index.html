About

This magnificent and awesome game was created as a web-project at the University of Applied Sciences Hagenberg. The images were created with a high end image editing tool called GIMP. The original images were downloaded from a small and rarely known search engine called Google.

I hope you enjoy the game have much fun! When you're bored and you have an idea to impreve the game you can help me develop it on GitHub.
